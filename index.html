Billy Beer

This class assignment involved a comprehensive rebranding endeavor for the legendary Billy Beer, culminating in the creation of a responsive website to showcase the brand and its various products, enhanced with dynamic content. The project primarily focused on infusing a fresh identity into the brand while preserving its historical significance.


During the initial stages, I took the lead in conceptualization, crafting sketch logos, and defining the brand's color palette. After thoughtful deliberation, we opted for a sophisticated blend of gold and blue, paying homage to the brand's original colors. To add a modern twist, we introduced new flavors, expanding the product range.


To visually communicate our vision, we used Cinema 4D to design eye-catching cans and bottles, injecting a contemporary feel into the brand's image. The subsequent step involved leveraging Adobe Creative Suite to create visually appealing posters and banners, aligning with the revamped brand aesthetics.


The final phase of the assignment was the website development, where tasks were collaboratively divided. I actively contributed to both the functional and design aspects of the site, employing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The end result is a seamlessly responsive website that encapsulates the essence of the rebranded Billy Beer, providing a dynamic and engaging user experience. This assignment serves as a testament to our collaborative efforts in rejuvenating a classic brand within the constraints of a class project.


Technologies Used:

HTML, CSS
Maxon Cinema 4D, Photoshop, Illustarator, Adobe XD

Project Contributions:


Web Designer (Dev Pandya) Crafted a succinct and impactful 15-second promotional video that revolved around the core concepts of "Create, Inspire, and Transform."Maintained the visual branding consistency with Fanshawe College, ensuring a professional and visually appealing presentation. Explored font choices, ultimately selecting Montserrat and Open Sans to achieve a minimalistic and professional look.

Key Phrases:

Dev's strategic choice of "Create, Inspire, and Transform" perfectly encapsulated the Industry Night event's essence, conveying the event's mission in just three words.

Visual Consistency:

By keeping the design consistent with Fanshawe College's brand identity, the website seamlessly integrated with the broader institutional image, while still maintaining a unique and visually appealing presentation.

Font Selection:

The experiment with fonts, Montserrat and Open Sans, led to the discovery of the perfect combination for a minimalistic and professional appearance. This choice ensured that the website's typography complemented the content, enhancing the overall user experience.

Project Outcomes:

Dev Pandya's contributions were instrumental in the successful creation of the website for Fanshawe College's Interactive Media Design program. The 15-second promotional video, with its three key phrases, effectively conveys the event's essence, while the visual consistency with Fanshawe College's brand and the thoughtful font selection contribute to a professional and engaging online presence.

Jujutsu Kaisen Wiki

The primary objective of the Jutsu Kaisen Wiki project was to create an informative and visually engaging web platform dedicated to the "Jutsu Kaisen" manga and anime series. The project aimed to provide fans with a centralized hub for character details, their special moves .

Role and Responsibilities:

As the sole contributor to the project, my responsibilities encompassed the design and implementation of a crucial interactive feature – the lightbox. The lightbox served as a key element for presenting detailed character information and additional content without requiring users to navigate away from the main page.

Key Contributions:

Lightbox Creation: Developed a customized lightbox using HTML and CSS to ensure an aesthetically pleasing and responsive overlay for displaying information. Integrated smooth transitions and animations within the lightbox to enhance the overall user experience.

Content Management: Structured content within the lightbox, prioritizing readability and accessibility. Applied CSS styling to elevate the presentation of textual information, maintaining a cohesive design approach.
